Canopy Resilience Project

The Canopy Resilience Project is led by the Centre for Water and Spatial Science (CWSS) at The University of Western Australia.

It brings together water scientists, geospatial data scientists, and science communicators to:

  • monitor the condition and health of Perth’s trees
  • advance understanding of how Perth’s trees respond to varying and drying climates
  • engage various stakeholders to support sustainable management of Perth’s green spaces
